01. Approach

We are one of many cleantech companies that started with one mission, but we have matured to become the one that can make it happen.

Everything started with the research of an established research professor who wanted to elevate his research to serve the world. This motivation brought together other like-minded individuals and established Squitex, the platform technology transforming the future of sustainable materials.

02. Squitex

Quite the opposite. We found a way to mimic the proteins found in squid using bio-fermentation and developed Squitex: a protein blend fiber with unique stretch, strength, and self-healing properties.

It is also sustainable and thermally responsive.

Squitex is more than just another material;

  • - Repairable with heat and pressure
  • - 100% biodegradable & recyclable
  • - Strong and elastic as spandex
  • - Thermally responsive
  • - Has low environmental impact
  • - Animal-alternative
